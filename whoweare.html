Who We Are

Our Vision

At Penstrokes for Progress, we envision a world where every artist has the resources, opportunities, and community they need to succeed. We believe in the transformative power of art and its ability to inspire, uplift, and bring people together.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Monthly Featured Artists: We showcase local artists in our vibrant newsletter, giving them a platform to share their work and stories.


  • Funding Opportunities: Through donations, auctions, and art classes, we raise funds to support artists financially.


  • State-of-the-Art Studios: Our in-person locations are equipped with advanced technology, including 3D printing, laser cutting, and 3D modeling, to help artists explore new mediums.


  • Colorful Community: Our diverse and dynamic community fosters collaboration, inspiration, and a sense of belonging among artists.


Join Us on This Creative Journey

Whether you're an artist looking for support or someone passionate about fostering creativity, we invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Together, we can make a positive impact on the world of art and provide opportunities for artists to thrive.
